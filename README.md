# 5pyider: Radioactive Ascension

**5pyider** is a high-octane, multi-dimensional arcade survival game. Control a radioactive spider, hunt flies to evolve, and dodge a relentless barrage of rocks from above as you attempt to transcend 2D reality.

### Evolution Path
1.  **The Hunt (Dimension 1)**: You start stationary. Catch **5 flies** to unlock the **AOE Web** and movement.
2.  **Survival**: Once movement is unlocked, rocks will begin to fall from the sky. Every **10 flies** caught increases the attack speed of the rocks.
3.  **The Portal**: Reach a score of **75** to open the Magenta Portal to the **Second Dimension**.
4.  **The Void (Dimension 2)**: A high-speed survival mode. You have **Infinite Web**, but the rocks are faster. Dodge **30 rocks** to open the final portal.
5.  **3D Ascension**: Enter the final portal to transcend into the **3D World**.

### Controls
*   **Left Click**: Shoot Web (Costs 1 Ammo in Dimension 1).
*   **Right Click**: Move to location (Costs 2 Ammo in Dimension 1).
*   **Context Menu (Right Click)**: Also used to enter Portals.

### Creatures
*   **Flies**: Standard prey. +1 Score, +2 Ammo.
*   **Golden Bugs (Rare - 2%)**: Shiny, fast, and high-value.
    *   **Reward**: +10 Score, +10 Web Ammo, and **Heals 10% Health**.
    *   **Health**: Takes only 1 hit to catch.

### Hazards
*   **Falling Rocks**: Target your location.
*   **Damage**: Each hit deals **25% Damage** to your health.
*   **Warning**: You get a 0.5s visual warning before impact.
